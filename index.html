Interests and Hobbies

I would call myself a gamer. I've been a gamer ever since I was a child. My starting game was Need for Speed: Most Wanted. As I grew older I became interested in various other genres of video games such as: First-person shooters, Open-World, and RPG games. As I became more interested I started watching gaming youtubers such as M3rkmus1c. Gaming will always be a passion of mine and something I use to relax and wind down at the end of the day.

I'm also a massive book reader. I've always been a bit of a nerd. I love Batman and DC Comics. I love to read the Batman comic books and graphic novels and learn more about the character. I have a habit of collecting the graphic novels and usually try to place them on my bookshelf on chronological order.
